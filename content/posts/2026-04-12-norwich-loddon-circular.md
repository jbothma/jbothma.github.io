Last weekend we cycled from Norwich to Loddon and back. This is a lovely route with many cute houses and pretty twists and turns.

I created a route on [OSMAnd](https://osmand.net/) from the [Norwich Cycle Map PDF](https://www.norwich.gov.uk/download/downloads/id/3488/norwich_cycle_map.pdf) before we set off. After a while I realised the route is sign-posted really well and I could probably just turn OSMAnd off and save phone battery. Still, it's fun to plan routes in OSMAnd now that I've figured out how to use it.

Download [Norwich Loddon Circular GPX](/routes/norwich-loddon-circular.gpx)

I probably got something wrong with the mapping on Mansfield Lane - I didn't see the sign posts - but we connected up with Lakenham and it wasn't really an issue.

Like I said - really cute:

[![Cute little corner along the route between Loddon and Norwich](/images/posts/PXL_20260406_110029020.jpg)](/images/posts/PXL_20260406_110029020.jpg)

[![The same place from the other direction](/images/posts/PXL_20260406_110212600.jpg)](/images/posts/PXL_20260406_110212600.jpg)

In Loddon we went for some refreshment at [The Angel Inn](https://www.theangelinnloddon.co.uk/). They had delicious beer and pizza on offer. Also some table games.

OSMAnd reckoned it'd take us about 3 hours. I think we ended up taking just over 2 hours each way, taking it really easy.

[![OSMAnd route analysis](/images/posts/Screenshot_20260412-133653.png)](/images/posts/Screenshot_20260412-133653.png)

This is definitely a route we'll do again, probably bringing friends along next time.
